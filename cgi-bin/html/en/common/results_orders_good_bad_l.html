Notes
Payments Due: The sum of all payments not captured and payment date before today.
Payments Paid: The sum of all payments already captured.
Payments Payable: The sum of all payments not captured yet, because they have a payment date subsequent to today.
Payments total: The sum of all payments regardless their Captured Status and payment date.
Good: The orders COD that do not have any payment due.
Bad: The orders COD that have at least one payment due.
